About Me자기소개

Hi, I'm Sunwoo Lee.
A developer who brings creative ideas to life through programming.

My journey began simply from the pure joy of playing games with friends. Over time, I fell in love with indie games that showcase unique narratives, art styles, and mechanics. I was especially drawn to the supportive indie culture and its communities. Feeling a deep sense of belonging in this collaborative space is what ultimately drove me to become a game developer.

I strive to be a developer who genuinely loves the game we are building, always keeping the player's experience at the heart of everything I do. I highly value a flat, collaborative culture where members can freely exchange diverse ideas regardless of their specific roles. By embracing seamless communication and constructive feedback, my ultimate goal is to work united as one team to craft a highly polished and memorable masterpiece.

To build the technical foundation needed to bring these visions to reality, I earned dual Bachelor's degrees: a B.S. in Computer Science from DigiPen Institute of Technology (Washington, USA) and a B.S. in DigiPen Game Engineering from Keimyung University (Daegu, South Korea).

My core technical expertise lies in C++ and OpenGL, utilizing Visual Studio 2022 and VS Code. Leveraging Unreal Engine 5, I have developed titles such as BEAR, a single-player 3D puzzle platformer, Busted!, a 3D multiplayer cops-and-robbers game, and more.
